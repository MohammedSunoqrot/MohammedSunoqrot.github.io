About Me

I am a dedicated researcher and data scientist with a PhD in medical technology and a background in biomedical engineering. My journey encompasses pioneering automated diagnostic methods for prostate cancer using MRI, complemented by expertise in machine and deep learning, data analysis, and innovation management. Navigating complexities, I've collaborated seamlessly across interdisciplinary teams, industry partners, and end users, resulting in patented technologies and adherence to stringent medical device regulations. My passion lies in harnessing data to drive advancements at the convergence of technology and healthcare. Join me in shaping the future of technology innovation.
